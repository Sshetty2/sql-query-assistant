Q1: Multi-Company Scan Summary (Medium)
===================================================================================
Show me a summary of scan activity for each company in the last 90 days, including total scans, full scans, and rescanned items

Expected: Multi-table join (Company, Scan), date filtering, aggregation, GROUP BY

Q2: High Severity CVE Computers (Medium-High)
===================================================================================
List all computers that have CVEs with CVSS score greater than 8.0, including company name, computer name, CVE name, and CVSS score

Expected: 4-table join (Company, Computers, CVEMap, CVE), numeric filtering

Q3: Pending Patches by Company (Medium)
===================================================================================
Show the top 5 companies with the most pending patches, ordered by count descending

Expected: 2-table join (Company, PendingPatch), aggregation, ORDER BY, LIMIT
